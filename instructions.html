Upload Photo and Signature Instructions

Photo Upload Instructions:
  1. Dimensions: 200 pixels width x 230 pixels height.
  2. File Size: 20 KB to 50 KB.
  3. Format: JPEG or JPG.
  4. Background: Light or white background.
  5. The photo should be a recent passport-size color photograph.
  6. Face should be clearly visible, facing the camera directly.
  7. Eyes should be open and visible.
  8. The photo should not have any borders.
  9. Ensure the photo is not too dark or too light.
Signature Specifications:
  1. Dimensions: 140 pixels width x 60 pixels height
  2. File Size: 10 KB to 20 KB
  3. Format: JPEG or JPG
  4. Background: White background
  5. The signature should be done on white paper with a black ink pen.
  6. The signature should be clear and without any smudges.
  7. Ensure the signature is not cropped too closely; there should be a margin around it.
